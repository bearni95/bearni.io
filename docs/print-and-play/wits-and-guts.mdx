# Wits & Guts

> Your All-In-One single-page TTRPG

<img class="project-icon" src="/img/projects/wits-and-guts.png"/>



Wits & Guts is a flexible and streamlined system built on the foundation of John Harper's 
iconic Lasers and Feelings. It’s a game of dualities—balancing precision and instinct, 
intellect and action, planning and improvisation.

With its minimalist mechanics and endless versatility, Wits & Guts serves as 
both a game in its own right and a creative framework for designing your own one-page RPGs.
